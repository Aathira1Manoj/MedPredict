MediPredict — Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
================================================

Q: What is MediPredict?
A: MediPredict is an AI-powered health awareness application that predicts possible diseases based on the symptoms you report. It also provides personalised diet recommendations and AI-generated meal plans tailored to your predicted condition, region, and food preferences.

Q: How does MediPredict predict diseases?
A: MediPredict uses a CatBoost machine learning model trained on thousands of real symptom-disease records. You enter your symptoms, age, and gender, and the model calculates the top 3 most likely conditions along with a confidence score for each.

Q: Is MediPredict a replacement for a doctor?
A: No. MediPredict is a health awareness tool, not a medical diagnostic service. The predictions are based on statistical patterns and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified doctor or healthcare provider for any health concerns.

Q: How accurate are the predictions?
A: The model achieves approximately 95% accuracy on its test dataset. However, accuracy in real-world use depends on the quality and completeness of the symptoms you enter. The more relevant symptoms you select, the better the prediction.

Q: What symptoms can I enter?
A: MediPredict supports over 130 symptoms including fever, cough, fatigue, joint pain, nausea, headache, skin rash, breathlessness, and many more. You can search or browse the symptom list during the assessment.

Q: How many symptoms should I select?
A: We recommend selecting at least 3–5 symptoms that you are currently experiencing for the most accurate prediction. More relevant symptoms generally lead to better results.

Q: What does the confidence score mean?
A: The confidence score is a percentage that indicates how strongly the AI model associates your entered symptoms with a particular condition. A higher score means the model is more certain. A low score (e.g., 12%) means other conditions are also quite plausible.

Q: Why are there three predictions instead of one?
A: Because many diseases share similar symptoms, it is important to consider multiple possibilities. MediPredict shows the top 3 most likely conditions so you and your doctor can make a more informed assessment.

Q: Where do the diet recommendations come from?
A: Diet recommendations are sourced from a curated clinical dataset mapped to each disease. The AI then generates a practical meal plan using only those clinically-sourced foods, adapted to your region and diet preference.

Q: Can I choose a vegetarian or non-vegetarian meal plan?
A: Yes. During the Details step, you can select either Vegetarian or Non-Vegetarian as your diet preference. The generated meal plan will strictly follow your choice.

Q: What regions are supported for diet plans?
A: Currently supported regions include Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Delhi/North India, West Bengal, Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Punjab. Meal plans are adapted to the local cuisine of the selected region.

Q: Is my health data stored or shared?
A: No. MediPredict does not store or share any of your health information. Your symptom inputs and predictions are processed in your session only and are not sent to any third-party service.

Q: Do I need to create an account to use MediPredict?
A: You can use the symptom checker without an account. However, creating a free account lets you save and review your prediction history across sessions.

Q: How do I view my prediction history?
A: After logging in, click on Predictions in the navigation bar or tap the profile icon on mobile. This will show all your saved predictions in chronological order.

Q: What is the About This Condition section?
A: After a prediction, MediPredict shows a plain-language summary of the top predicted disease — what it is, common causes, and what you should know. This is generated by an AI language model to help you understand the condition better.

Q: Can children use MediPredict?
A: Yes. The age range supported is 1 to 120 years. The model uses age as a feature in its predictions, so results may vary across age groups. For children, always consult a paediatrician.

Q: What if none of the predicted diseases match my actual condition?
A: MediPredict is not infallible. If the predictions do not seem relevant, you may have a condition not well-represented in the training data, or you may not have selected the most characteristic symptoms. In such cases, please see a doctor.

Q: Is MediPredict free to use?
A: Yes, MediPredict is completely free to use for individuals seeking health awareness and dietary guidance.

Q: Which AI model generates the meal plans and summaries?
A: MediPredict uses the Qwen 3 32B language model via the Groq API for fast, high-quality text generation. The model is prompted to stay strictly within clinically-sourced diet data and to avoid making unsupported medical claims.

Q: What should I do in a medical emergency?
A: If you are experiencing a medical emergency such as chest pain, difficulty breathing, stroke symptoms, or severe injury, do NOT use MediPredict. Call emergency services (112 in India) or go to the nearest hospital immediately.
